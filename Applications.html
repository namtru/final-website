Recommendations

To address the growing risks of deepfake AI, the following measures are recommended:

  • Develop Advanced Detection Tools: Invest in machine learning systems capable of identifying deepfakes with high accuracy to mitigate their misuse.
  • Public Awareness Campaigns: Educate individuals on recognizing deepfake content to reduce the spread of misinformation.
  • Strengthen Legal Frameworks: Implement policies to penalize malicious use of deepfake technology while supporting ethical and beneficial applications.
  • Ethical AI Development: Encourage AI developers to integrate ethical considerations and safeguards into the design of deepfake tools.
